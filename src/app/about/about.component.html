About the Autobahn App

Discover the latest roadworks and highway updates with the Jacob-Autobahn-App. Easily navigate through different Autobahn sections, view road conditions, and access real-time map data for a seamless travel experience.

This app provides users with detailed information about roadworks, including location, road conditions, and available facilities like parking and charging stations. Our aim is to enhance your driving experience by keeping you informed about the Autobahn network.
